Philately

Kiribati

I collect stamps and currency from the Republic of Kiribati (pronounced ki-ri-bas), a remote island nation in the central Pacific Ocean. I am particularly interested in stamps related to the Rawaki Phoenix Islands group.

Odd-shaped stamps

I am interested in non-rectangular stamps from around the world. I have created a website to document a selection of odd-shaped stamps.

Bermagui Local Post

I am the postmaster and founder of Bermagui Local Post (BLP). BLP produces stamps and delivers mail within Bermagui. Learn more at bermaguilocalpost.org.
